Services

At MB Connectivity, we provide a comprehensive range of specialized services, with a strong emphasis on transport modelling. These projects form the foundation for data-driven decision-making across transport policy, masterplanning, sustainable urban mobility planning, and network design. Leveraging advanced technologies and in-depth industry expertise, we deliver customized solutions that promote efficiency, sustainability, and innovation in every engagement.

Travel Demand Modelling

We develop advanced models to predict travel patterns and assess infrastructure needs, enabling our clients to plan effectively for future growth, optimize resource allocation, and enhance overall transportation system performance.

Micro Simulation

Our microsimulation services deliver high-resolution traffic flow analysis, enabling precise optimization of road networks, intersections, and overall transport system performance.

Geospatial Analytics

We leverage geospatial data to uncover critical spatial relationships, providing actionable insights that support strategic planning, informed decision-making, and effective resource allocation.
